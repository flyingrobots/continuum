# Integration Scenario Corpus

## Idea

Continuum could own a small corpus of golden cross-repo scenarios:

- same authored contract through Wesley
- consumed by `warp-ttd`
- backed by Echo and `git-warp`
- checked through witness and compatibility surfaces

## Why It Is Interesting

This would let us test the stack at the seams instead of only inside each repo.

## Not Yet

Do not build a huge fixture empire first. Start with one boring shared family and
one debugger-visible scenario.
